What is Web3

The third iteration of the third sort of stage of the web

WEB1.0:
只能讀取某人放上網站的資料 read-only web

WEB2.0:
Become interactive,Facebook,Twitter,Instagram
If you are not paying for it. You are the product. We can read,and we can also write,we are interacting with the website.

WEB3.0:
區塊鏈,虛擬貨幣,與虛擬貨幣結合的APP

How does the Blockchain actually work?

交易平台上面的交易紀錄,包含金額,都是要夠過加密讓紀錄不能輕易的變動

使用HASH function進行加密,同樣的密碼會得到同樣的HASH(64個字母),轉換成HASH很容易,但是要解碼卻非常的困難

把所有的交易相關資訊進行HASH,this is how a block in the blockchain is formed.
We create some information,in order to indentify that infomation, we are going to have to generate a hash and the hash becomes the indentifier for this block.

In order for a block to be added to the blockchan,it has to satisfied some predetermined requirement.

例如:我設定我的hash前三個字母要是000,我的Nonce會是3883,這個Nonce只針對這組資訊有效,資訊內容只要稍微有變動,整組block就會變成是無效的block

What we are doing with our ledger is we’re taking the data that we want to add into the ledger(比喻為交易平台),and we are puttung in the each of the blocks by hashing the data with a nonce.

假設我想更改資料內容怎麼辦呢?

幾乎是無法做到的

稍微修改block1的資料,整組blockchain就會無效果,we can not change anything that goes into the blockchain,otherwise it is going to invalidate all of our hashes,ans it is no longer satisfy the requirements.

除了以上的方式來加密,區塊鏈還有一個更進一步的方式

Distributed Blockchain

We have three miners on the distributed blockchain.

Every time a new miner joins the blockchain,they are going to get a full copy of the blockchain what means is that three people have the same blockchain that they hold.

某一個人的資料被改了,就算他從新remine一組,他的hash也跟其他的人不一樣了,the network can tell the player has become a bad player,so by distributing the blockchain on mutiple computers,we now have another way ensuing the integrity(誠實) to make sure that our data is absolutely correct and nobody has tampered(竄改) with it.

We have a distributed trustless ledger,we got the blockchain,which is actually encodes all of the thansactions

Smart contract

The real break through here is they realized that not only can we store data inside each of these blocks,who owes who money or who has how much money, we can create what is called smart contracts by storing code into the blocks.

在block裡面寫上程式碼,當金錢的達到一定的數量,就會轉成虛擬貨幣給接收者

What are DApps(Decentralised apps)and how to develop

Old way

You had first build your start-up,build the technology,deploy it, then raise VC funding (is capital that you get from investment groups that work with startups and small businesses),if they like your idea,like your product,and you would at some point down the line, if you are really lucky,maybe IPO(Initial Public Offerings) and cash out

Web3 way

In this case,you are buliding your start-up,you are building the technology,but then you can already start releasing tokens to the public or funds like VCs and you can start selling tokens that enable people to use and interact with your technology,you are able to raising monry from Dapp day one,and the way that you run your company is also transformed by the blockchain because you can form what is called these decentralized autonomous(自主的) organizations(DAO), which kind of replace the (*board of director(董事會) as well as the entire organization chart of a company.

So you can have people who are token holders They are special tokens called governance tokens,which allows you to vote on what the company or the Dapp should do in the future.So in these DAOs,you are able to start controlling how the company functions and you allow users to contribute the road map of you decentralized application.

And in a way,everybody’s incentives are aligned,because if the app does well,then the token price will go up,and become more valuable.And in order to do that,the company has to do valuable things,which means that you have to make good decisions,so everybody in the DAOs is incentivized to participate and try and help the organization along.

pseudonymity

完全的匿名性,只在乎你做了什麼(APP的功能性),年齡,性別都不是問題
